Examples

1. Airbnb (www.airbnb.com):

Cozy Colors: They use soft blues and greens that make you feel relaxed. Easy to Read: The text stands out against the background, so it's easy to read. Important Stuff Stands Out: Buttons and important things are a different color, so you notice them first.
2. Spotify (www.spotify.com):

Fun and Bright: Their colors are bright and fun, matching their vibe. Feelings in Colors: Each part of the website feels different because of the colors, like happy or calm. Easy for Everyone: Even with bright colors, it's still easy to see everything.
3. Slack (www.slack.com):

Clean and Fancy: Their colors are soft and look fancy. All the Same: The colors are the same everywhere, so you know it's Slack. Little Things Help: They use colors for little signs that make it easy to use.
These websites show how colors can make websites feel different and help you understand things better. Airbnb feels cozy, Spotify feels fun, and Slack feels professional. They all use colors to help you know where to look and what to do.
